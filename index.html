Rock Paper And Scissors

Hand signals:

Players use hand signals to represent rock, paper, or scissors:
Rock: A closed fist
Paper: A flat hand with fingers and thumb extended, palm facing down
Scissors: A fist with index and middle fingers fully extended

Winning

The first player to score 3 or more points wins.

Beating

Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. If both players throw the same hand signal, it's a tie.
